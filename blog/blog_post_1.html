Personal apocolypse

A man dies on a street in downtown Ottawa
A lifetime of sensory experiences flash by
Holding a finger so big your hand could not encircle it
Beams of sunlight holding warm snowflakes
Naked feet on hot pavement chasing rabbits
The newer stuff that somehow feels furthest away
All wrapped up into a singularity that subsequently disappears forever
A personal apocolypse takes place in the days, weeks, and years before the end
A night as loud as every other, and yet no one heard a thing
